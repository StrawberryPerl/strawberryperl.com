Strawberry Perl Release Notes

Version 5.8.9.4, released 28 Jan 2010

Check out what is new, what known issues there are, and frequently asked questions about this version of Strawberry Perl. As always, you're encouraged to tell us what you think.

What's new in Strawberry Perl 5.8.9.4: Collapse

A new update to Strawberry Perl!

  • All modules updated to current versions as of the end of January.
  • GDBM_File and Test::Exception have been added to the included modules.
  • Internal preparations for "Strawberry Professional" (beta version should be coming out soon)

Known issues: Collapse

  • No known issues yet. Identified issues will be linked here as they occur.
